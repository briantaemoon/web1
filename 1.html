P2P Business 이란 무엇인가?

P2P business model is not only a risk free but also sizable business. Airbnb, Lending Club, Uber are example of successful P2P business model. In order to understand P2P business model, we have to understand P2P economy.

P2P economy is a decentralized model whereby two individuals interact to buy or sell goods and services directly with each other, without an intermediary third-party, or without the use of a company of business. The buyer and the seller transact directly with each other.
